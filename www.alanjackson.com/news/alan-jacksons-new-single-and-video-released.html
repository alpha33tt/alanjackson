ALAN JACKSON’S NEW SINGLE AND VIDEO RELEASED


"So You Don't Have To Love Me Anymore"

NASHVILLE, Tenn (Jan. 18, 2012)  Country music superstar Alan Jackson’s new single, “So You Don’t Have To Love Me Anymore,” is at radio now and the video for the song started airing on CMT and GAC this week.  You can also view the video at http://youtu.be/B7MbZJZ0biQ. The single is from the forthcoming debut album from the joint venture between ACR (Alan’s Country Records) and EMI Records Nashville.

The video, directed by award-winning Steven Goldmann, was shot at Coney Island in New York City.
