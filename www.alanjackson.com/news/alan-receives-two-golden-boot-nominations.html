ALAN RECEIVES TWO GOLDEN BOOT NOMINATIONS

The Golden Boot Awards are back!


The Boot’s fan-voted digital awards return in 2015 for a second year, and this time around, we’ve the Living Legend Award.  Alan’s been nominated for both Living Legend and Live Act of the Year. Each category features staff-selected nominees who made waves in the country music world from May 2014 through April 2015.


You can vote as often as once per hour in the poll below through noon ET on Monday, June 8, and we’ll announce the winners on the site.
