THE TOP 15 AJ LOVE SONGS

by alanjackson.com

Undeniably, Alan’s most popular love song is Remember When.  Over the years he’s recorded some great love songs.  Most of them happy, a few of them sad, but they’ve all been about love. Just in time for Valentine’s Day, we’ve complied a list of The Top 15 AJ “LOVE” songs.  We hope you have a wonderful Valentine’s Day and are surrounded by everybody you love!

1. I’d Love You All Over Again

2.  Love’s Got a Hold On You

3.  You Can’t Give Up on Love

4.  Everything I Love

5.  I’ll Go On Loving You

6. It Must Be Love

7. When Somebody Loves You

8.  I Slipped and Fell In Love

9. If Love Was a River

10.  First Love

11. When The Love Factors High

12. I Could Get Used To This Lovin’ Thing

13.  So You Don’t Have To Love Me Anymore

14.  Once in a Lifetime Love

15.  Never Loved Before
