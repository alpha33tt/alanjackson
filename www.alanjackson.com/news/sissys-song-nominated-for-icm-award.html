Sissy's Song Nominated for ICM Award

Sissy's Song Nominated for ICM Award

The Christian Country Music Association announced its nominees for the this year's Inspirational Country Music Awards.  "Sissy's Song" has been nominated for Mainstream Inspirational Country Song of the Year and Video of the Year (Director Scott Scovill). 

The award show  will be held on Thursday, October 15, 2009, at 7:00 p.m. CST at the Trinity Music City USA Auditorium located in Hendersonville, Tennessee. The member-voted ICM Awards Show is the keynote event of Inspirational Country Week, slated for October 11-15, in Nashville, TN. The convention and show are presented by the 501C-3 not-for-profit corporation, founded in 1992, The Christian Country Music Association.
