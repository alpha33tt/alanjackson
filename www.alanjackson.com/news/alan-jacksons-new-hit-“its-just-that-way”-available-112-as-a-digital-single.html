ALAN JACKSON’S NEW HIT, “IT’S JUST THAT WAY,” AVAILABLE 1/12 AS A DIGITAL SINGLE

Nashville, TN - Fans are already falling in love with Alan Jackson’s new single, “It’s Just That Way,” and beginning tomorrow (Wednesday, 1/12), they’ll be able to own their own copy with the January 12th release of the digital single. Currently inside the country radio Top 40 and moving fast, “It’s Just That Way” is the first hit from Jackson’s next album, arriving this spring.

 

Jackson’s most recent collection, 2008’s acclaimed Good Time, launched three #1 smash singles, bringing his career tally to an amazing 34 chart-toppers, 24 of which he wrote himself.

 

Fans who haven’t yet heard “It’s Just That Way” on their favorite country station can also hear it right now on alanjackson.com.  LISTEN NOW

 

Available through iTunes, Amazon.com and Walmart.com.
