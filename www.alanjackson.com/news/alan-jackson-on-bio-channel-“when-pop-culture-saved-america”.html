ALAN JACKSON ON BIO CHANNEL “WHEN POP CULTURE SAVED AMERICA”

Airs Monday, September 5

NASHVILLE, Tenn.  (Aug. 31, 2011)  Country music superstar Alan Jackson will be featured on “When Pop Culture Saved America,” a two-hour documentary on the A&E Biography Channel honoring the 10th anniversary of 9/11. The special takes a comprehensive look at the days and months following the attacks on 9/11 to show how the non-news media pitched in to bring the nation together.  Jackson was requested to be a part of the special because of the soul-touching effect “Where Were You (When The World Stopped Turning)” had on the nation.  “When Pop Culture Saved America” airs Mon., Sept. 5 at 8:00pm ET on the A&E Biography Channel.
