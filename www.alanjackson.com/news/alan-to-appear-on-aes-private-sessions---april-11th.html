Apr  11 , 2010

Alan to appear on A&E's Private Sessions - April 11th!

 
This  Sunday April 11 @9AM (8c) A&E's Private Sessions is excited to welcome superstar Alan Jackson! The entire hour is on Alan, where he sits down with host Lynn Hoffman for 30 minutes and is quite chatty, discussing the new release, his early years, moving to Nashville, etc. He performs 5 songs: Chattahoochee, Good Time, Where Were You (When the World Stopped Turning), plus 2 from Freight Train: Every Now And Then, and Hard Hat and A Hammer.   
 
And he's also surprised by Martina McBride, Lee Ann Womack, Glen Campbell and Chris Daughtry.
