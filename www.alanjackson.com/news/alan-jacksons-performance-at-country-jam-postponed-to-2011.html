Alan Jackson’s Performance at Country Jam Postponed to 2011

Alan Jackson’s Performance at Country Jam Postponed to 2011

June 15, 2010 – Grand Junction, Co - Country Jam USA has announced that Alan Jackson's 2010 performance has been postponted to the 2011 festival.

“We regret any inconvenience this change may cause, “ said Julie Schaefer Marketing Director for the event, “but Alan Jackson fans can look forward to seeing him perform at Jam on Saturday, June 25, 2011.”

Miranda Lambert, who just won three ACM Awards including Top Female Vocalist of the Year, Album of the Year, and Video of the Year and who has had the #1 country song in the nation for the last three weeks with “The House That Built Me,” will now headline the festival on June 26th. Crowd favorite Clay Walker has also been added to Saturday’s line-up. Walker’s latest single, “She Won’t Be Lonely Long,” is a current top-five hit on the country charts and his new album of the same name was just released earlier this month.

Current ticket holders have several options due to the scheduling change:

·         Keep their tickets for the show (General Admission)

·         Exchange their tickets for a different day at Country Jam (VIP and RESERVED seats)

·         Exchange their tickets for tickets to the 2011 festival

·         Request a refund by calling 1.800.780.0526

 

Country Jam USA is a four-day country music festival that will take place from June 24-27 just outside of Grand Junction in Mack, Colorado. Additional stars scheduled to perform include Keith Urban, Trace Adkins, Billy Currington, Sawyer Brown, Gloriana, Lee Ann Womack, Jake Owen, Big Kenny, LoCash Cowboys, Danny Gokey, and more. Four-day passes and single-day tickets are still available by calling 1-800-780-0526 or by going to www.countryjam.com

For additional information please visit www.countryjam.com
