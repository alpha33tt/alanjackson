Chris Young Blog: On the Road with Alan Jackson

Chris Young Blog: On the Road With Alan Jackson

Posted: April 12th, 2010 at 4:04 pm  |  By: Chris Young for cmt.com

Editor's note: Chris Young will be blogging each week from the road while he's opening shows on Alan Jackson's Freight Train tour with Josh Turner.

Just got off of the stage in Greenville, S.C., and man it was awesome! This is the fourth day of the Freight Train Tour -- and all four days were great. You guys blew me away with the standing ovations! The fact that I got a standing ovation all four nights of the tour so far is truly amazing. I was not expecting that response, and you have no idea what that means to me. Josh Turner and Alan Jackson both put on a great show, and I'll try to sneak you some details about that next week! Alan actually pulled me up on his bus today to say "hi." It's an awesome feeling to be welcomed by one of my heroes. And let me tell you, he has a ridiculously cool bus, and it looks amazing on the inside. I love my bus, but his definitely has more video screens. We'll see if he lets me try out my Wii system on there! haha. It's looking like this is gonna be a great tour! Hope to see you out there -- CY
