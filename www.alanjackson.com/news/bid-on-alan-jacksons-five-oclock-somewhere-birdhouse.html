Bid on Alan Jackson's Five O'Clock Somewhere Birdhouse

Bid on Alan Jackson's Five O'Clock Somewhere Birdhouse

ALAN JACKSON: It should come as no surprise that Alan's house echoes the laid back sentiment of his hit song, "It's Five O'clock Somewhere."  The beach-inspired house is painted in turquoise and pink and features a row of flip flops strung across the back of the house, a comfy Adirondack chair at the front door, his signature and a palm tree on one side of the roof and the song's title on the other.  


Who This Auction Benefits
The net proceeds from this auction benefit the W.O. Smith Music School in Nashville. The W.O. Smith/Nashville Community Music School, founded in 1984, is a nonprofit educational institution created for the purpose of making quality music instruction available to talented, interested, deserving children from low income families at the nominal fee of 50 cents a lesson. The school also seeks to encourage student participation in the cultural life of the community through concert attendance and performance. To learn more, visit www.wosmith.org.
