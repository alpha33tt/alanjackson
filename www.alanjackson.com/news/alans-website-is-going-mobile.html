ALAN'S WEBSITE IS GOING MOBILE

The next time you log on to Alan's website from your smartphone, you'll have the option to download our brand new Mobile Roadie App!  It's like a mini-AJ website just for your phone.  With this app you’ll be able to connect with friends, check-in at shows, download music & photos, have access to exclusive content and so much more!  We might even give away some pretty cool prizes for people using the app!!

**To log into your Fan Club account, you'll still have to go to the main site
