VOTE for ALAN in GAC's Top 50 Videos of 2011

You Decide Who Makes the Countdown

VOTE daily for LONG WAY TO GO and up to 9 more of your favorite videos.

Videos released between 11/1/10 and 10/31/11 are eligible for this year's voting. The nominees can be sorted by artist name or song name. Just click "add" or drag up to 10 of your favorite videos, in any order, to the ballot then click on the "Submit Your Ballot" button to place your vote. Voting ends on Nov. 30, 2011 at 5pm ET!  The countdown begins on Monday, December 26 at 8/7c.

GAC is also giving you the chance to win $5,000 in cash as a ‘thank you’ for voting. Enter our Top 50 Videos Sweepstakes after you place your vote and you could be kicking off the new year with a little extra spending money. The sweepstakes ends December 12.
