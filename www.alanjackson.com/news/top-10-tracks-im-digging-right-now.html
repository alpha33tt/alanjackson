Top 10 Tracks I'm Digging Right Now

Top 10 Tracks I'm Digging Right Now
Posted: March 1st, 2010 at 12:23 pm  |  By: Alison Bonaguro  

Alan JacksonWell, this was damn near impossible -- trying to narrow down my love of Alan Jackson to 10 measly tracks. Do I choose ballads over two-steppin' tunes? Old ones over new ones? It's so hard to restrict myself. But when he announced last week that he was coming to Chicago on May 1, I immediately went to my vast collection of Jackson music and started my wish list for what he'd play. Not only is he one of my favorite country boys to see in concert, but this show will be the first big one of the live country season. So he marks, for me, the beginning of a spring and summer full of music I love.

"Working Class Hero"
"Who's Cheatin' Who"
"Tall, Tall Trees"
"Buicks to the Moon"
"Everything I Love"
"Drive (For Daddy Gene)"
"Livin' on Love"
"Chasin' That Neon Rainbow"
"Where I Come From"
"A Little Bluer Than That"
